Name: Kim Koo (金九)

Major Achievements and Contributions:
  • Served as the President of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Korea: He led the Provisional Government during the Japanese colonial period, overseeing independence movements both domestically and abroad, and dedicated himself to the liberation of Korea.
  • Independence Activist: He was active in organizations such as the Sinminhoe and the Korean Patriot Corps, fighting for the nation's self-reliance and independence throughout his life.
  • 'Baekbeomilji' (My Wish): Through his writing 'My Wish' in his book 'Baekbeomilji,' he expressed his aspiration for a completely independent and culturally strong nation, deeply impressing many citizens.

(optional) any other information you think is relevant
  • His pen name was Baekbeom (白凡), meaning that even ordinary people should have patriotism.
